Welcome!

I am a cellist based in New York City, currently pursuing an Artist Diploma at The Juilliard School under the guidance of Astrid Schween and Joel Krosnick. Previously, I received my Masters in Music from Juilliard and a Bachelors in Computer Science at Columbia University, as part of their exchange program. I grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area, and was a student at the San Francisco Conservatory of Music Pre-College Division and a member of the San Francisco Symphony Youth Orchestra.
