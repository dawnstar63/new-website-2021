Biography

Elena Ariza is rapidly establishing herself as one of today’s most creative and community-driven cellists. Elena was a featured cellist in Yo-Yo Ma’s groundbreaking Music Art Life project, a guest artist on NPR's national radio show From the Top, and has performed with luminaries such as Itzhak Perlman, Michael Tilson Thomas, Christopher O’Riley, and more.
